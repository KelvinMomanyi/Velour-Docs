Scentra Theme Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for the Scentra theme. Scentra is a premium, high-performance Shopify theme engineered for the modern eCommerce landscape. Built entirely on Shopify's Online Store 2.0 (OS 2.0) architecture, Scentra offers unprecedented flexibility, blistering speeds, and deep integration with Shopify's most advanced features.

Shopify Theme Store 2026 Compliant This theme has been rigorously tested and successfully meets all accessibility, performance, and functionality benchmarks mandated for the 2026 Shopify Theme Store submission requirements.

2. Getting Started

This section outlines how to install the Scentra theme onto your Shopify store and begin the customization process.

Installation

  1. From your Shopify Admin dashboard, navigate to Online Store > Themes.
  2. In the Theme library section, click Add theme and select Upload zip file.
  3. Choose the scentra.zip file provided with your purchase.
  4. Once uploaded, click Customize to open the Theme Editor and begin setting up your store.

3. Theme Settings

Theme Settings control the global styles and behaviors of your store. Changes made here apply across all templates unless overridden by specific section settings.

Colors

Scentra utilizes an advanced Color Scheme system to guarantee contrast and accessibility.

  • Background & Gradients: Set the foundational background colors for your sections.
  • Text & Links: Control the primary reading colors. We recommend maintaining high contrast against your backgrounds.
  • Buttons: Assign solid backgrounds for primary Calls to Action (CTAs), and outline colors for secondary interactions.

Typography

Shopify's native font picker is deeply integrated into Scentra.

  • Heading Font: Applied globally to all <h1> through <h6> tags.
  • Body Font: Applied to standard paragraph text, button labels, and navigation items.
Performance Tip Using native Shopify fonts ensures typography is loaded via a global CDN asynchronously, protecting your Lighthouse performance scores.

Social Media

Link your brand's social profiles in the Theme Settings. These links automatically populate social icons found in both the Header and Footer sections, and configure the necessary OpenGraph meta tags for sharing products online.

4. Header & Navigation

The Header is managed as a global Section Group, meaning modifications apply uniformly across your entire storefront.

Navigation Menus

The header naturally supports deeply nested drop-down navigation. To configure:

  1. Go to Online Store > Navigation to build your link structures.
  2. In the Theme Editor, click on the Header section.
  3. Assign your newly created navigation list to the Menu setting.

Localization Forms

Scentra features native Country/Region and Language selectors directly inside the Header and Footer. If you utilize Shopify Markets, these dropdowns will automatically populate based on the regions you have enabled in your store Admin settings.

5. Home Page Sections

Thanks to JSON templates, you can add dynamic sections anywhere on your homepage (and across the site). Featured sections in Scentra include:

  • Hero/Banner: High impact imagery. Supports focal points to ensure your focal subject is never cropped out on mobile screens.
  • Gallery: A dynamic image layout grid. Click on images to zoom or enter full-screen modals.
  • Featured Collection: Display a horizontally scrollable or grid-based layout of a specific collection.
  • Video: Seamlessly integrate autoplaying or user-controlled video elements without impacting render-blocking scripts.
  • Custom Liquid For advanced users, the Custom Liquid section allows you to inject raw code or utilize 3rd-party App Blocks securely into the grid.

6. Product Pages

The Product template is the conversion engine of Scentra. It is entirely modular.

Product Blocks

Inside the Theme Editor, expand the Main Product section to see available blocks. You can reorder, duplicate, or hide elements like:

  • Title & Price: Supports unit pricing natively for EU compliance.
  • Variant Picker: Can be displayed as dropdowns or visually appealing color swatches. Color swatches pull automatically from the variant terminology.
  • Buy Buttons: Supports both standard Add to Cart actions and Accelerated Checkouts (Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay).
  • Shop Pay Installments: Dynamically displays split payment banners if enabled on your gateway.
  • Pickup Availability: Shows customers if the product is available at physical retail locations based on your Shopify Admin locations.

Cross-Selling

At the bottom of the product page, Scentra includes built in Complementary Products and Related Products sections. These leverage Shopify's core Recommendation API to serve highly relevant alternate products to customers automatically.

7. Collection Pages

Your catalog browsing experience is incredibly robust.

Filtering & Sorting

Scentra utilizes Shopify's Storefront Filtering API. You do not need external apps to filter by Price, Color, Size, or Availability.

  • Enable filtering in the Theme Editor by clicking on the Product Grid section.
  • Note: Filters are driven by product variants and Metafields. Ensure your products are meticulously tagged in the Admin to get the most out of filtering.
Focal Point Cropping When products display in a grid, Scentra respects the "Focal Point" set on images within your Shopify Admin > Files area. Your products will always look perfectly balanced.

8. Cart & Checkout

Cart Types

Scentra supports both a dedicated Cart Page and an intuitive Cart Drawer. You can toggle this behavior via Theme Settings > Cart.

  • Cart Notes: Allow customers to leave special instructions for their order.
  • Tax & Shipping Disclaimers: Required legal and tax verbiage automatically displays adjacent to the subtotal.

Gift Cards

The theme provides a highly polished gift_card.liquid template natively. When a customer receives a gift card, they can:

  • Copy the code instantly.
  • Scan a generated QR Code with a mobile device.
  • Add the gift card directly to Apple Wallet.

9. Localization & Global Selling

Scentra is built for international audiences. It supports full translation hooks and cross-border selling structures natively.

Multiple Languages & Currencies

Ensure that Shopify Markets is configured. Scentra's form selectors will automatically capture the customer's intent and reload the page with the appropriate request.locale and currency codes applied without requiring external routing applications.

10. Developer Guide

For agencies and developers extending Scentra, the architecture follows strict ES-module loading patterns and utility-first CSS principles.

CSS Architecture

Core structures reside in application.css.liquid, while critical above-the-fold layout CSS is inline injected into the theme.liquid `` to prevent Flash of Unstyled Content (FOUC). All component-specific styles (e.g., component-predictive-search.css) load asynchronously.

JavaScript

Scentra heavily utilizes <script type="importmap"> and strict Module Preloading. Global behaviors like throttling, debouncing, and event trapping are housed in global.js and the ScentraUtils window object.

App Blocks (@app)

Third-party apps are natively supported in Scentra everywhere that commerce requires them. The primary main-product and featured-product grids explicitly yield block rendering for {%- when '@app' -%} components, ensuring seamless plug-and-play app installations via the Shopify Editor.
